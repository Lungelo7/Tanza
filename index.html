Tanzarella Live is a Broadcasting Studio Channel which is hosted by Dr Gherto F. Tanzarella Sr. The studio is currently located in South Africa at the North West Province. The aim of the Broadcast Channel is to teach, preach of the Gospel of Jesus Christ and His resurrection and also teach about the power and significance of the conversation with the Holy Spirit.
